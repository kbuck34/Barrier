Our Story

Adopted by the
Lowcountry

We weren't born here, but we fell in love with South Carolina when we moved here 19 years ago. The Lowcountry has a way of doing that — and once it gets you, you want to take care of it.

That's what Barrier Island Exterior Cleaning is about. Coastal homes face challenges that most cleaning companies don't understand — salt air, humidity, black algae, and the relentless pull of the marsh. We use soft-wash systems calibrated specifically for the exteriors, rooflines, and surfaces common throughout the Lowcountry.

What We Do

Complete Exterior Care

Four specialized services — each refined for the salt-air climate and architectural character of the Lowcountry coast.

🏠

House Washing

Our signature soft-wash system removes mold, mildew, algae, and salt deposits without a single PSI of damaging pressure. Your siding, paint, and trim emerge like-new.

  • Soft-wash (no high-pressure damage)
  • Biodegradable cleaning agents
  • All siding types: Hardie, vinyl, wood
  • Eaves, soffits & fascia included
  • Pre-rinse of landscaping
🛤️

Driveway Cleaning

Oil stains, mold, mildew, and years of coastal grime are no match for our surface cleaning system. We restore concrete, pavers, and brick to a finish that elevates your entire property's first impression.

  • Concrete, paver & brick safe
  • Oil & rust stain treatment
  • Algae & mildew elimination
  • Walkways & pool decks included
  • Sealing services available
✨

Window Cleaning

Crystal-clear windows restore your Lowcountry views. Our purified water system leaves zero streaks, spots, or residue — inside, outside, or both.

  • Purified water-fed pole system
  • Interior & exterior available
  • Screen cleaning & reinstallation
  • Track & frame detail cleaning
  • Hard water stain treatment
🍃

Gutter Cleaning

Clogged gutters are the hidden culprit behind fascia rot, foundation seepage, and landscape erosion. We clear, flush, and inspect every linear foot — then bag and haul everything away.

  • Full debris removal & haul-away
  • Downspout flush & check
  • Gutter brightening available
  • Before & after photos provided
  • Seasonal maintenance programs

How It Works

A White-Glove
Process

From first contact to final walkthrough, every step is designed to feel effortless for you and exacting for us.

I

Complimentary Quote

Submit your details online or call us directly. We'll assess your property — often with a visual survey — and provide a transparent, itemized quote within 24 hours. No surprises.

II

Pre-Service Preparation

We notify you 48 hours ahead. Our technicians pre-wet and protect all landscaping, seal outdoor outlets, and reposition anything that could be affected — before a single drop of solution touches your home.

III

Precision Cleaning

Using calibrated soft-wash rigs and biodegradable detergents, we work methodically from roof to foundation. Every surface receives the specific treatment its material requires.

IV

Final Walkthrough & Guarantee

Before we leave, we walk the property with you — or send a complete photo report if you're not on-site. Everything we service is backed by a 30-day satisfaction guarantee.
